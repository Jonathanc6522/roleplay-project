Character Creation

When making a character, it can be overwhelming, but it's important to take things one step at a time.
You can use D&D 5E wikidot to start looking at what you can do.



Above is the standard example of a character sheet. You should start by filling out race, class, and the 6 stats. (ability scores)

Personally, I think magical classes are hard to understand, so I wouldn't recomment it as your first try. Another thing I will say, is that sometimes a campaign will have preset characters where you have control over the creative aspect, while the technical is already done for you.

It's important to note that every DM (dungeon master) may have different world rules based on what kind of campaign they run. (I, for example, run magicless, sci-fi short stories!)
